IEEE Industry Applications Society

One of the largest special interest societies within the institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers(IEEE), the IAS focuses specially on the unique needs of industry and commerce. IAS is a source of professional power to its nearly 14,000 worldwide members.

Mission

IEEE Industry Applications Society enables the advancement of theory and practice in the design, development, manufacturing and application of safe, sustainable, reliable, smart electrical systems, equipment and services.

IEEE Industry Applications Society will be a world leader in the advancement of science and technology linking theory and practice in the application of electrical and electronic systems for the benefit of humanity.

ABOUT MAR ATHANASIUS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering pioneers engineering education in central Kerala since 1961. Managed by Mar Athanasius College Association, Aided by the Government of Kerala, the college was first in Asia under Christian Management. The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It now has six full fledged departments. IEEE IAS SBC MACE is one of the most dynamic student branch chapter with a total membership count of 271. This student branch chapter is very active and has conducted various events for the growth and development of its members. We have a strong enthusiastic team of IAS Society volunteers, volunteering at various levels. Due to the persistent efforts put in by the team, they have been recognized at various levels.

ABOUT IEEE MACE SB

IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE student branch was established in MACE in the year 1988, since then it established it's hallmark in all concentration levels of IEEE. IEEE SB at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering is one of the largest and promising student branches in Kerala. It is a dedicated to shape innovative and high-quality events. IEEE MACE SB offers access to technical innovation, cutting-edge information, and networking opportunities. The SB has a student volunteer count of 540 and its Executive Committee (Execom) comprises 40 members. Both the technical and informal events are conducted frequently by its various Society chapters and the programs are organized at various levels – Execom level, College level, Zone level, and State level. Innovative ideas and passion are often fecilitated via its open meetings. The strength of our SB is its spirited student volunteers who have proven themselves on many occasions.
